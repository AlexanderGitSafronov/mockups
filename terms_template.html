Last updated August 17, 1993

Hobbytown USA is a small mom and pop sign shop with a much bigger vision for the future. We want our culture here to be as important as the quality of work we put out. Our company is fortunate to have some of the greatest customers and we are growing due to word of mouth. We need talented people who care about the quality of work we put out. Our job is not just to make signs, it is to help our customers advance their business through their visual merchandising.

Looking for an intrapreneur to start out at a base level and grow with us. Companies can only grow with quality people working with them. The more we work on the mission to grow together, the more we can offer in return.

Job Description:

-Reading work orders and keep production flowing through the shop from printing/cutting through to packaging for shipment or customer pick up.

-Loading media on printer and plotter/ Running machines. (Large format printer, plotter, laminator, flatbed printer)

-Must be able to handle multitasking and quick turnover

- Needs to be good and working with your hands and accustomed to using tools and being on ladders

-Will occasionally be asked to assist with installs outside of shop.

*** If you are interested, please send a cover letter on why you think you would be a good fit, resume, and some digital copies of your work. Does not need to be professional, can be hobby work as well. Looking to see personality/skill level.
